Who Are We ?

Laundry Shark is a modern laundromat that is revitalizing the industry. Our company is here to help our customers get their laundry done efficiently in a safe and clean environment. Our superior levels of customer satisfaction are made possible by our laundry professionals. No change? No problem! Laundry Shark understands that consumers today have more choices, more mobility, and less time than ever before. This is why we have made it possible and extremely easy to accept mobile payments so that our customers receive the fastest and most convenient laundry experience that fits seamlessly into each customers’ lives. We’re Dry Cleaning Specialists with the Best Quality and Service! Laundry shark has been providing Amman and Irbid communities with exceptional Dry Cleaning and Laundry services for over 3 years. Established in 2019 by Sharks Group, Laundry shark has since grown to become the premier dry cleaner in Jordan What makes us different from other dry cleaners? We are family owned and operated business, and we take pride in our work. Whether it’s a big presentation at the office, a family wedding, or a night at the opera, when you look your best, so do we. All work is done right on our premises in Amman. This allows us to deliver the highest level of service and quality to you, our customer. We make garment care easy! Laundry shark wants to make garment care easy.
